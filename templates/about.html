About Meridian University

Discover our rich history, mission, and commitment to excellence in education

Our Mission

At Meridian University, we are committed to fostering intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and innovative problem-solving. Our mission is to provide transformative educational experiences that prepare students to be leaders, innovators, and responsible global citizens.

Core Values

  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in teaching, research, and service
  • Innovation: We embrace creativity and forward-thinking approaches
  • Integrity: We act with honesty, transparency, and ethical responsibility
  • Diversity: We celebrate and learn from our diverse community
  • Service: We are committed to serving our community and society

Our History

1952

Foundation

Meridian University was founded with a vision to provide exceptional higher education and contribute to societal advancement through research and innovation.

1965

Graduate Programs

Expanded to offer graduate degrees, establishing our reputation as a research-intensive institution.

1988

Technology Center

Opened state-of-the-art technology and research facilities, positioning us at the forefront of innovation.

2010

Global Expansion

Launched international partnerships and study abroad programs, creating a truly global educational experience.

2020

Digital Innovation

Pioneered hybrid learning models and digital education platforms, leading the transformation in higher education.

Our Campus

Research Labs

Student Housing

Modern dormitories and apartments providing comfortable living spaces for our residential students.

Accreditation & Recognition

Meridian University is fully accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and holds specialized accreditations from leading professional organizations. Our commitment to quality education is recognized nationally and internationally.

  • Higher Learning Commission (HLC)
  • AACSB International (Business Programs)
  • ABET (Engineering Programs)
  • Commission on Collegiate Nursing Education (CCNE)
  • National Association of Schools of Music (NASM)
